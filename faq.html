Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Who can donate blood?

A: Generally, individuals aged 18-65, in good health, and meeting specific weight and health criteria can donate blood.

Q: How often can I donate blood?

A: Donation frequency depends on the blood donation center guidelines, but it's typically every 8-12 weeks.

Q: Is blood donation safe?

A: Yes, blood donation is safe. The equipment used is sterile and disposable, ensuring no risk of infection.

Q: Can I schedule appointments online?

A: Absolutely! Our platform allows users to easily schedule appointments for blood donation at their convenience.
